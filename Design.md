Design Style: Modern • Premium • Minimal • Enterprise • Glassmorphism

---

# Design Philosophy

Kristen Technologies should look like a premium enterprise technology company comparable to Microsoft, Stripe, Linear, Vercel, Framer, Notion, and Apple.

The entire UI should emphasize:

- Spacious layouts
- Soft premium shadows
- Rounded corners
- Elegant typography
- Large hero sections
- Beautiful illustrations
- Smooth animations
- Accessibility
- Consistent spacing
- Professional trust-building design

# Overall Design Goal

Every page should communicate:

• Premium Enterprise Quality
• High Trust
• Modern Technology
• Elegant Simplicity
• Excellent Readability
• Strong Visual Hierarchy
• Beautiful Motion
• Consistent Branding
• Seamless Light/Dark Experience

The final product should feel like a premium SaaS website suitable for an international technology company while maintaining Kristen Technologies' blue-and-green brand identity.
